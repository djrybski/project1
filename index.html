My Love for Fishing & Technology

I was always an outdoor child growing up. I would immerse myself in nature I was always in the yard digging up worms or catching spiders.

Eventually I got into fishing and my obsession with the outdoors skyrocketed. I was riding my bike out of my Southside neighborhood to the suburbs to fish whenever I could.

Eventually I got my license and a car. With this came more fishing. When I was 17 I took a Computer Science class.

Like fishing, coding was a lot of problem solving and I loved that. I realized that if I set my mine to it, I could bring technology & fishing together

I went away to college at Indiana Tech to play baseball and study computer science I chose Indiana because it had a decent program and three rivers to fish right by the school

Go figure fishing had a role to play in one of the biggest decisions of my life Once I realized that I needed to come back to Chicago, I knew IIT was the best option

I am working on a fishing App right now with another student I met here I also fish the 31st Harbor every chance I get to sneak away between classes
